Creamy cheesecake

Prep time: 20 minutes

Yield: 45

Ingredients






Steps:

Biscuit base

  1. Mix the crushed biscuits and melted butter and press down into a 22cm tart dish or spring-form tim. Refrigerate while making the filling

Filling

  1. Whisk the Cremora and the cold water together until all lumps have disappeared. It starts off looking very lumpy and crumbly. Just whisk a bit more and it will beome more liquid.

Tips

  • For a more flavoured cheesecake, you can add chocolate, or fruit puree to the filling.
  • Letting the cheesecake cool gradually helps reduce cracks on the surface.
